Star Wars Zodiac Website

This was another class project I took on. The goal was to create a responsive Horoscope webpage from scratch using HTML & CSS, incorporating Javascript to make the site fucntional. I chose to do a Star Wars character theme as I am an avid fan of the franchise.

Designing the Site

I first mocked out a rough idea of what I wanted from the interface and what functionality I hoped to give my page, from hover states to sound effects. This planning was important as I got deeper into my development and often served as a roadmap for my tasks.

I regards to the actual design, since this was more of a fun project I opted for a more fun look that paid homage to Star Wars in a satisfying way.

The Final Design

I was very happy how my horoscope site came out for my first time working with JavaScript. I was able to accomplish everything I set out to do with the project and look forward to working with JavaScript again soon!
