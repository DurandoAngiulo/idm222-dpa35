JA Electric Site Design

This was a smaller freelance project I undertook. I was offered to design a website for a local residential electrician Company called JA Electric. The company was based on a referral system and wanted a website so they could expand their customer base.

Designing the Site

I used SquareSpace to design and host this site. The software itself is simple to understand and made it easy for me to build the elements I wanted.

When actually designing the site, I opted to go for a clean, modern, visual aesthetic. I wanted a professional feel behind the site so I kept things minimalist, but still tried to maintain engagement across the different pages.

The Final Design

The client and I were both really happy with how the final site turned out! The company now has a fully functioning site that promotes their business, and makes it easy to get in contact with them.
